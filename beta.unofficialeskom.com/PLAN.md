- Local check: `yarn start`, click through Heatmap, FAQ index, FAQ → Diesel, About, Long-term snapshots.
- Deploy with the standard `rsync -av --delete build/ hh:/var/www/beta.unofficialeskom.com/` (see [AGENTS.md](file:///Users/g/git/personal/eskom/unofficialeskom_v2/beta.unofficialeskom.com/AGENTS.md)).
