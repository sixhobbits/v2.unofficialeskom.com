Interruption of Supply and Manual Load Reduction looking good. I always forget exactly what these are an how they relate with each other

![chart](https://cdn.bsky.app/img/feed_fullsize/plain/did:plc:nrgklo4jfeofuhg2x7ta5p6x/bafkreidxlpzrlezs3gczzuelw6v5csv26saqdkzaxb7n2qrzaxyrxdcjfu)

> IOS – Interruption of Supply: all contracted as well as mandatory demand reduction resources utilised by Eskom National Control. Includes IOS due to Transmission network faults.

> MLR – Manual Load Reduction. An estimation of demand that has been reduced due to load shedding and/or curtailment.

so super clear? Anyway, nice that they're low.

wait there are three

> ILS – Interruptible Load Shed. This is consumer load(s) that can be contractually interrupted without notice or reduced by remote control or on instruction from
Eskom National Control. Individual contracts place limitations on usage.
